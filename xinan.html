云南茶山方言
Yunnan Tea Mountain Dialect

所处位置：云南茶区
Location: Tea-growing regions, Yunnan

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危中，茶业现代化
Endangerment: Moderately endangered, modernization of tea industry

发音特征：音调复杂，可能受闽语影响
Phonetic Features: Complex tones, possible Minnan influence

词汇特征：茶业词汇，如“普洱”地方表达
Lexical Features: Tea-related vocab, e.g., local “Pu’er”
