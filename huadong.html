The following is a list of endangered Chinese dialects in the East China region:

畲话
She Dialect

所处位置：福建、浙江、广东
Location: Fujian, Zhejiang, Guangdong

濒危程度和保护现状：子方言濒危，约70万使用者
Endangerment: Endangered sub-dialect, ~700k speakers

发音特征：可能与客家相关，音系受多方影响
Phonetic Features: Possibly Hakka-related, multi-influenced

词汇特征：畲族文化词汇，如“山歌”变体
Lexical Features: She culture terms, variant of “mountain song”

龙都方言
Longdu Dialect

所处位置：福建龙岩
Location: Longyan, Fujian

濒危程度和保护现状：子方言濒危，闽语发音
Endangerment: Endangered sub-dialect, Minnan base

Phonetic Features: Typical Minnan pronunciation, multiple tones

词汇特征：客家闽语混合词汇，如“土楼”地方表达
Lexical Features: Mixed Hakka/Minnan terms, e.g. local “tulou”
