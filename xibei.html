The following is a list of endangered Chinese dialects in the Northwest region:

甘肃丝绸之路方言
Gansu Silk Road Dialect

所处位置：甘肃古丝绸之路城镇
Location: Towns along the ancient Silk Road in Gansu

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危高，旅游冲击，保护有限
Endangerment: Highly endangered, tourism impact, limited cultural protection

发音特征：保留古汉语元素，受中亚语言影响
Phonetic Features: Retains archaic Chinese elements, influenced by Central Asian langs

词汇特征：贸易相关词汇，如“驼队”地方表达
Lexical Features: Trade terms, local word for “camel caravan”

青海高原方言
Qinghai Plateau Dialect

所处位置：青海高原牧民区
Location: Pastoral areas on the Qinghai Plateau

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危中，游牧生活变化
Endangerment: Moderately endangered, nomadic lifestyle changes

发音特征：高原环境影响，受藏语影响
Phonetic Features: Plateau influence, Tibetan elements

词汇特征：游牧词汇，如“牦牛”变体
Lexical Features: Nomadic terms, e.g., variant of “yak”

陕北偏远方言

Location: Northern Shaanxi
