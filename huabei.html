The following is a list of endangered Chinese dialects in the North China region:

冀东偏远方言
Remote Eastern Hebei Dialect

所处位置：河北东部
Location: Eastern Hebei

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危高，农业社区减少，保护少
Endangerment: Highly endangered, shrinking agricultural communities, minimal protection

发音特征：卷舌音突出，冀鲁官话特征
Phonetic Features: Prominent retroflex, Hebei–Shandong features

词汇特征：农业词汇，如“地瓜”地方称呼
Lexical Features: Agricultural terms (e.g., local “sweet potato”)

山西偏远村庄方言
Remote Shanxi Village Dialect

所处位置：山西省多地
Location: Various parts of Shanxi

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危高，入声保留
Endangerment: Highly endangered, retains entering tone

发音特征：晋语特征，音调复杂
Phonetic Features: Jin dialect traits, complex tones

词汇特征：煤炭相关词汇，如“煤窑”变体
Lexical Features: Coal-related vocab, variant of “coal mine”

内蒙古偏远方言
Remote Inner Mongolia Dialect

所处位置：内蒙古东部
Location: Eastern Inner Mongolia

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危中，受蒙古语影响
Endangerment: Moderately endangered, Mongolian influence

发音特征：辅音丰富，音系多样
Phonetic Features: Rich consonants, diverse system

词汇特征：游牧词汇，如“牧草”地方表达
Lexical Features: Nomadic terms, e.g., local “forage”

京郊村庄方言
Beijing Suburban Village Dialect

所处位置：北京郊区
Location: Suburban Beijing

濒危程度和保护现状：严重濒危，普通话冲击
Endangerment: Critically endangered, overshadowed by Mandarin

发音特征：官话特征，轻声明显
Phonetic Features: Mandarin-based, noticeable light tones

词汇特征：农耕市井词汇，如“胡同”变体
Lexical Features: Agriculture & alleyway terms, variant of “hutong”

天津偏远方言
Remote Tianjin Dialect

所处位置：天津郊区
Location: Tianjin suburbs

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危中，港口相关词汇，无官方保护
Endangerment: Moderately endangered, port-related vocab, no official protection

发音特征：音调平缓，辅音清晰
Phonetic Features: Smooth tones, clear consonants

词汇特征：海河港口词汇，如“码头”地方表达
Lexical Features: Port terms, local word for “wharf”

鲁西北方言
Northwestern Shandong Dialect

所处位置：山东西部
Location: Western Shandong

濒危程度和保护现状：濒危中，农业渔业社区减少
Endangerment: Moderately endangered, declining agrarian/fishing communities

发音特征：卷舌音突出，声调较少
Phonetic Features: Prominent retroflex, fewer tones

词汇特征：农业渔业词汇，如“黄河”相关表达
Lexical Features: Agriculture/fishing terms, references to Yellow River
