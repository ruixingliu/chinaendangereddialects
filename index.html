千言千寻 —— 记录濒危汉语方言的最后回声

Dialects@Chinese – Preserving the Last Echoes of Endangered Chinese Dialects

我们的使命 / Our Mission

在中国广袤的土地上，千百年来孕育了众多风格各异、韵味独特的方言。它们不仅是交流的工具，更是承载历史、文化与身份认同的活化石。然而，在现代化、城市化及标准普通话推广的浪潮下，这些方言正以惊人的速度消失。一旦消失，它们承载的文化记忆、民间智慧、地方传统，也将一同湮没在时间的洪流之中。

Across the vast lands of China, numerous dialects with distinct styles and rich tones have flourished for centuries. These dialects are more than just communication tools; they are living fossils of history, culture, and identity. However, with rapid modernization, urbanization, and the promotion of Standard Mandarin, these dialects are vanishing at an alarming rate. Once they disappear, the cultural memories, folk wisdom, and regional traditions they embody will be lost forever.

千言千寻（Dialects@Chinese）旨在搭建一个专注于中国濒危汉语方言的线上档案馆。我们希望通过对方言的地理分布、语音语法特征、特色词汇、保护现状等方面的深入记录，为它们留下数字时代的语言化石，让未来的研究者、语言爱好者和普通公众都能听见它们的声音。

Dialects@Chinese is designed to serve as an online archive dedicated to China's endangered Chinese dialects. By meticulously documenting their geographical distribution, phonetic and grammatical characteristics, unique vocabulary, and preservation status, we aim to create a linguistic fossil record in the digital era—ensuring that future researchers, language enthusiasts, and the general public can still hear their voices.

关注汉语方言，而非少数民族语言
Focus on Chinese Dialects, Not Minority Languages

中国拥有极其丰富的语言生态，除了汉语方言体系，还有55个少数民族的语言。然而，千言千寻主要聚焦于汉语方言的记录与保护，而未涵盖少数民族语言。这是因为，少数民族语言往往有独立的语言体系，而汉语方言虽然彼此差异巨大，却仍然被视为汉语的不同变体。

China has an incredibly diverse linguistic landscape, including the Chinese dialect family and the languages of 55 ethnic minorities. However, Dialects@Chinese focuses exclusively on Chinese dialects, rather than minority languages. This distinction arises because minority languages often have independent linguistic systems, whereas Chinese dialects—despite their vast differences—are still considered variants of the Chinese language.

此外，许多少数民族语言已受到国家及国际机构的关注，相关研究较为系统，部分甚至有较完整的书写系统和官方保护措施。而汉语方言的保护相对薄弱，部分方言甚至在未被详细记录前就已消亡。因此，我们希望将有限的精力投入到汉语方言的数字化存档，使这些濒危方言能够在消失之前留下尽可能完整的语言学痕迹。

Moreover, many minority languages have received attention from governmental and international organizations, with systematic research, established writing systems, and official preservation efforts. In contrast, Chinese dialects often lack formal documentation, and some vanish before being properly recorded. With limited resources, we prioritize the digital preservation of endangered Chinese dialects, ensuring they leave behind a trace before disappearing forever.

尽管我们无法覆盖所有中国的语言，我们仍然鼓励更多的人关注少数民族语言的保护，并欢迎任何与汉语方言相关的资料贡献。

While we cannot encompass all languages spoken in China, we encourage more efforts in preserving minority languages and welcome contributions related to Chinese dialects.

为什么要保护方言？
Why Should We Preserve Dialects?

方言不仅仅是日常交流的工具，它们见证了历史的变迁，承载了地方的独特文化。许多方言中包含了古汉语的遗存，隐藏着独特的语音和表达方式，同时也蕴含着一个地方的民俗、传说、诗歌和故事。

Dialects are more than just tools for communication; they are living witnesses to historical changes and carriers of regional culture. Many dialects preserve elements of Classical Chinese, contain unique phonetic patterns and expressions, and encapsulate local folklore, legends, poetry, and stories.

保护方言，就是保护文化的多样性。每种方言的消失，都是人类文化版图上的一次不可逆的消减。我们希望千言千寻能成为记录这些珍贵声音的数字博物馆，让它们的韵律和故事得以被更多人听见、理解、传承。

To preserve dialects is to safeguard cultural diversity. Every lost dialect is an irreversible erosion of humanity’s cultural landscape. We hope Dialects@Chinese will serve as a digital museum, capturing the melodies and stories of these dialects so that they can be heard, understood, and passed on.

让我们一起聆听，记录，守护这些濒危的语言！

Join us in listening, documenting, and protecting these endangered dialects!
